So, You're Here!

Welcome to the Superphone Zone. If you're here, it means you followed a link or a QR code and are most likely interested in what we have going on here.

You're in the right place, you've officially taken your first step into becoming a tube mind in a square world, a rock of consequence in a sea of unaccountability.

Look around, watch, read and listen to what we have to offer, and remember. It's For You.

 

Who Are We?

Superphone is a New-Wavey Synthpop Band from Columbus, Ohio.

We take influence from a variety of bands and groups, but most notably Devo, Men at Work and They might be Giants

Lyrically and thematically, our songs are pretty all over the place, but even when tackling serious topics we try to have fun.

 

What Do We Do?

We make music, as well as videos. We pride ourselves in being a "multimedia" band, so to speak.

The Youtube channel linked above contains not just songs, but music videos, behind the scenes content, and comedy sketches.

We run a dial-a-song service updated most Wednesdays, hosting demos, unused tracks, and covers

 

Why?

This is a complex question with no concrete answer. But I can say with certainty that it's fun, and other people enjoy it.

It's also an excellent outlet to deal with emotions and the constantly changing square headed world around us
