2600:

2022

The first Superphone EP, written and recorded between late 2021 and early 2022. Contains the Cabinet Man cover, 2 original tracks, and demos for all 3.


 

Listen:

Bandcamp  Youtube  Spotify  Mini-CD

 

Trivia:

  • Superphone alumni Mr Coffee was instrumental in shaping the final versions of these tracks, the demos were what I presented to him in December or so of 2021, and by March he had reshaped them into the pleasant soundscapes presented on the EP!

 

  • The EP art is based on an old statistics software sleeve from the late 1970s.          Many of my designs for Superphone can be traced back to contemporaries,           I like to call it visual sampling!
