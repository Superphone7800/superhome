Trivia:

  • The earliest traces of what became this album began to take shape in May of 2025, with an early version of Hold Your Ground appearing on a charity album for Nesey Gallons, and early versions of Going Horizontal and Digital TV Dinner being performed live that September in Chicago

 

  • The name "Functional Music" comes from a 1970s Philips Background Music tape system, seen here being reviewed and repaired by Techmoan
