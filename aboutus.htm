First, Some History:
Superphone is a project I started in 2021 as a way to finally express my musical genes. I'd been writing music since I was in elementary school, and when I bought a secondhand Omnichord that June I knew it was time to make my move. That month, during a trip to California I parked the Superphone Bandcamp page. It lay dormant for a while until late September after an extended stay in the hospital that was exceedingly boring. During the time I had alone with my feelings I vowed to do something once I got out, make a cover of Cabinet Man. The gears were in motion, I scooped up 2 of my friends and we were on our way. The rest, as they say, is history.

 

Who is Superphone?

Hello, I'm Bea Thurman, and I'm the one responsible for this mess. I'm 21 years old, and this is my passion project. When I'm not working on Superphone I can be found tinkering with old technology, participating in local activism, and playing Mr Do at the local arcade.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

What's a Tube Mind?

The idea of Tube Mindedness refers to my personal philosophy. In life, there are two kinds of people. Tube Minds and Square Heads. Being tube minded allows new ideas to flow freely though your mind, without disturbing the walls of your core values if necessary. Square heads are sharp, confrontational, edgy, and un-aerodynamic, and in this wind tunnel of a world, you gotta roll with the punches or you'll get blown away.
