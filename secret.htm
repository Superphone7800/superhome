What is the Super Secret Society?

Long ago, bands had things called "fan clubs". Where listeners, for a fee, could sign up to be given a poster of the lead singer's face or something. The Super Secret Society is more than that.

Membership to the society is free to those who seek it, and benefits are included with any and all physical media purchases.

 

Take a look around, you're on the inside now.
