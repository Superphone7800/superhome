Who are We?

Superphone is a "Wonky" rock band based around the continental US, consisting of 4 key members (Bea Thurman, "Sneeps" Coffee, Josh Gruin, and Matthew "Molgera") and a wide array of supporting characters. We specialize in vaguely lo-fi and experimental sound based around early digital synthesis, as well as analog and acoustic sounds sprinkled in for good measure.
