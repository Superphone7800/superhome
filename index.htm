What Is Superphone?

Superphone is a New-Wavey Synthpop band from Columbus, Ohio.

We take a mix of crunchy samples, Midwest Synthesis, and unique, unorthodox, and otherwise unpalatable sounds and shove them in the musical food processor. Creating a rich, creamy, blend of new and old.
