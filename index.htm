Who Are We?

Superphone is a synthpop band headquartered across the continental United States, Consisting of 4 key members.

Bea Thurman, "Sneeps" Coffee, Josh Gruin, and Matthew "Molgera". The band is supplemented by wide cast of friends, assosciates, and other types of supporting characters! We specialize in lofi, experimental, and crunchy digital sounds and synthesis, as well as analog and acoustic sounds sprinkled in for good measure.
